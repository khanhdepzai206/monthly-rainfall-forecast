‚ÑπÔ∏è Model Details & Methodology

Gradient Boosting (XGBoost): Ensemble learning method that builds trees sequentially. Uses advanced feature engineering including seasonal encoding (sin/cos), lag features, and moving averages. Optimized via Grid Search for hyperparameter tuning.
SARIMA (Seasonal ARIMA): Time series forecasting model specifically designed for seasonal data. Decompose series into (AR: autoregressive, I: integrated, MA: moving average) + seasonal components. Automatically validated using ADF test for stationarity and ACF/PACF for parameter selection.
LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory): Recurrent neural network architecture that captures long-term dependencies in sequential data. Includes dropout regularization to prevent overfitting. Suitable for complex non-linear rainfall patterns but requires more training data.
Evaluation Metrics:
  • MAE: Mean Absolute Error - average magnitude of prediction errors (mm)
  • RMSE: Root Mean Squared Error - penalizes larger errors more heavily (mm)
  • R¬≤: Coefficient of determination - proportion of variance explained (0-1 scale)
  • AIC: Akaike Information Criterion - trade-off between fit and model complexity
